Blockwiz · Web3 Marketing Agency
30 B2B Case Studies in 30 Days for a Crypto Agency
45%

The Challenge
Blockwiz needed a rapid content sprint to build credibility in the crowded Web3 space. They had the results but no structured stories to show for it — and a client pipeline that stalled at the proof stage.
The Approach
Built a repeatable interview-to-publish framework. One case study per day: structured client interviews, narrative architecture, outcome mapping, and SEO-optimised publishing — all without sacrificing depth or voice.
The Result
45% lift in client onboarding within 60 days of publication. The library became the centrepiece of their new business pitch — social proof that converted cold leads into signed contracts.
02 ★
BetterInvest · Fintech
Organic Retention Campaign That Drove ₹8.3 Cr in Fresh Investment
₹8.3Cr
›
The Challenge
An existing investor base going dormant. No paid media budget. The goal: reactivate lapsed investors and drive new capital commitments through content and lifecycle messaging alone.
The Approach
Designed a 3-phase retention email sequence mapped to investor psychology. Paired it with educational long-form content addressing investor anxieties, and a LinkedIn thought leadership campaign for the founding team.
The Result
₹8.3 Cr in fresh investment across 2 months. Zero paid media spend. The campaign proved that narrative-led retention could compete with performance marketing — and outperform it on ROI.
